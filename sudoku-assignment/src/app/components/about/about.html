🧩 About Sudoku A puzzle with a surprising history

📜 History

Sudoku’s roots go back to an 18th-century Swiss mathematician and later an American puzzle magazine in the 1970s. Japan popularized it in the 1980s under the name “Sudoku”, which means “single number”.

🌍 Global Popularity

By the early 2000s, Sudoku exploded worldwide — newspapers, books, and eventually apps everywhere. It became a daily brain workout for millions.

💡 Fun Fact

Even though the grid is only 9x9, there are 6,670,903,752,021,072,936,960 possible Sudoku solutions. That’s 6.67 quintillion!
